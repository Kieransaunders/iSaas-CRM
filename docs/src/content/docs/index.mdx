## What is iSaaSIT?

iSaaSIT is a **SaaS starter pack for agencies** who want to build **client portals**—giving their customers a secure, self-service platform to access project data, documents, and collaborate with your team.

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Client Portal Ready" icon="users">
    Launch a branded client portal where your customers can log in, view their projects, download files, and communicate
    with your team.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Multi-Tenant Architecture" icon="cloud">
    Manage all your clients from one platform with complete data isolation. Each client only sees their own data.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Role-Based Access Control" icon="lock">
    Three built-in roles—Admin (you), Staff (your team), and Client (your customers)—with automatic data scoping.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Authentication Ready" icon="approve-check">
    Enterprise-grade auth with WorkOS AuthKit. SSO, magic links, and social login supported out of the box.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Modern Stack" icon="rocket">
    Built with TanStack Start, Convex, React 19, Tailwind CSS v4, and TypeScript for rapid development.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Billing Integration" icon="credit-card">
    Monetize your client portal with Polar integration. Usage-based billing and subscription management included.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
