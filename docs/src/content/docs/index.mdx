## What is iSaaSIT?

iSaaSIT is a **CRM SaaS starter kit for agencies** who want to build **client CRMs**—giving their customers a powerful relationship management platform to track contacts, deals, tasks, and communications.

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Contact Management" icon="users">
    Organize and manage all your client contacts in one place. Track details, notes, and communication history.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Deal Pipelines" icon="seti:json">
    Visual sales pipelines to track opportunities through stages. Drag-and-drop deal management.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Task Management" icon="check">
    Assign and track tasks across your team. Set deadlines, priorities, and follow-ups for every deal.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Multi-Tenant Architecture" icon="cloud">
    Manage all your clients from one platform with complete data isolation. Each client only sees their own data.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Role-Based Access Control" icon="lock">
    Three built-in roles—Admin (you), Staff (your team), and Client (your customers)—with automatic data scoping.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Authentication Ready" icon="approve-check">
    Enterprise-grade auth with WorkOS AuthKit. SSO, magic links, and social login supported out of the box.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Modern Stack" icon="rocket">
    Built with TanStack Start, Convex, React 19, Tailwind CSS v4, and TypeScript for rapid CRM development.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Billing Integration" icon="credit-card">
    Monetize your CRM with Polar integration. Per-client billing, usage caps, and subscription management included.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
